How to Make Pasta

Making Pasta can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

This pasta recipe is beloved for its bold flavors and versatility, making it easy to customize. Stick to the original recipe for an unforgettable experience or tweak it to suit your preferences. Here's what you'll need:

  • Pasta: Any type of pasta (spaghetti, penne, fusilli, etc.)
  • Olive oil: For cooking and flavor
  • Garlic: Minced, for aroma and taste
  • Tomatoes: Fresh or canned, for the sauce
  • Basil: Fresh leaves, for garnish and flavor
  • Parmesan cheese: Grated, for topping
  • Salt: To season the pasta water and sauce
  • Black pepper: For seasoning
  • Optional protein: Grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu

How to Make Pasta Step-By-Step Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade pasta:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add salt to the water for seasoning.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta, reserving about 1 cup of the pasta water, and set aside.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  5. Add minced garlic to the pan and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  6. Add tomatoes (fresh or canned) to the pan and cook until softened, creating a sauce.
  7. Season the sauce with salt, black pepper, and optional herbs like basil.
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing to combine. Use reserved pasta water to adjust the sauce's consistency if needed.
  9. Serve the pasta hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
